Activities I like

I like to swim, cycle and run. I am in training for my first triathlon, but it is hard work.

Kinds of Triathlon There are different kinds of triathlon = sprint, Olympic and so on. I am aiming on Olympic, which consists of the followling:
  1. 1.5km swim
  2. 40km cycle
  3. 10km run
